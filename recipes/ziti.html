Baked Ziti

Baked Ziti: A truly delcious dish that can be made within an hour. From the sauce to the melted cheese, this dish is truly for everyone. Below you will find the necessary ingredients to make this truly delicious recipe!

Ingredients

  • Ziti noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Ground meat
  • Grated Parmesan

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil
  2. Add ziti noodles once water has reached a boil
  3. In a separate pan; cook ground meat
  4. Once ground meat and noodles are cooked place to side for later use
  5. Grab a baking dish that is suitable for use in the oven
  6. Add tomato sauce to bottom of baking dish (just enough to cover the bottom)
  7. Place noodles on top of sauce
  8. Add ground meat
  9. Spoon out 1 cup of ricotta cheese
  10. Break mozzarella cheese into tiny bits and spread across the dish
  11. Add the rest of the tomato sauce
  12. Sprinkle parmesan cheese on top
  13. Place in oven at 350 degress for 1 hour
