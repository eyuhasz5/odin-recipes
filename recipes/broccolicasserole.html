Cheesy Broccoli Casserole Recipe

Broccoli casserole is a quick but delcious dinner to make for the family or bring to your next potluck dinner with friends.

Ingredients

  • Broccoli
  • Cream of mushroom soup
  • American Cheese
  • Ritz crackers
  • 1 Egg

Steps

  1. Steam the broccoli
  2. Coat the bottom of a baking dish with the cream of mushroom soup
  3. Add steamed broccoli to baking dish
  4. Place loose strips of American Cheese across the baking dish
  5. Mix in egg
  6. Crush Ritz crackers (while still in packaging) spread crushed crackers on top
  7. Place in oven at 350 degrees for 1 hour
